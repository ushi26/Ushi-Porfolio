This poster series explores the invisible yet powerful forces beneath the surface of water, using the human hand as a metaphor for vulnerability, resistance, and connection. Each piece pairs bold typography with fragmented imagery to represent different states of being when submerged—physically, mentally, and emotionally. DISSOLVE – Symbolizes the loss of boundaries and the liberation that comes with letting go. The hand dissolves into texture, reflecting the idea of freedom in fluidity. UNDERTOW – Represents unseen forces that pull us in directions we cannot control. The grasping hands evoke tension and struggle against invisible currents. STILLNESS – Captures the paradox of silence and pressure. The frozen gesture suggests that peace is not emptiness, but a heavy stillness that holds everything in place. DEPTH PRESSURE – Embodies the intensity of going deeper, where weight and sensation amplify. The upward-reaching hand illustrates both burden and transformation under pressure.
